An Azure Reserved Virtual Machine Instance is an advanced purchase of a Virtual Machine for one or three years in a specified region.
The commitment is made up front, and in return, you get up to 72 percent price savings compared to pay-as-you-go pricing.
Reserved Virtual Machine Instances are flexible and can easily be exchanged or returned.
Azure Virtual Machines gives you the flexibility of virtualization for a wide range of computing solutions with support for Linux, Windows Server, SQL Server, Oracle, IBM, SAP, and more. 
All current generation Virtual Machines include load balancing and auto-scaling at no cost
